About StayZee.com

Who?

StayZee.com is an online accommodation platform created to help forigners find safe, comfortable and affordable homestays and rooms anywhere in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to make staying away from home easier and more enjoyable by connecting travellers with trusted property owners.

What is the purpose?

Travellers and students manytimes struggle to find safe homestays at the right price. Our platform connects verified homeowners with customers for safe and affordable booking.

Why Choose Us?

  • Verified listings
  • Real photos
  • Secure bookings
  • Customer support
  • No middleman fees
  • Rooms sharing option

Who will benifit?

  • Travellers(forigners) visiting Sri Lanka
  • students
  • Remote workers
  • Families on vacation
